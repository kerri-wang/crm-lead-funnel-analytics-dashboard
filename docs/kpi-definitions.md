# KPI Definitions

## Conversion Rate
**Definition:** Won Deals / Total Leads

**Business meaning:** Measures overall end-to-end lead conversion performance.

## Qualified Rate
**Definition:** Qualified Leads / Total Leads

**Business meaning:** Measures how effectively raw leads are turning into meaningful opportunities.

## Win Rate from Qualified
**Definition:** Won Deals / Qualified Leads

**Business meaning:** Measures how efficiently qualified opportunities convert into final wins.

## Follow-Up Rate 24h
**Definition:** Leads contacted within 24 hours / Total Leads

**Business meaning:** Tracks responsiveness and early-stage lead handling discipline.

## Cost per Qualified Lead (CPL)
**Definition:** Campaign Spend / Qualified Leads

**Business meaning:** Measures campaign efficiency based on qualified opportunities rather than raw lead volume.

## ROI
**Definition:** Revenue / Spend

**Business meaning:** Evaluates campaign return relative to marketing investment.
